Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH) offers academics opportunities in technological oriented fields. Academics programmes offer by the university span different field of specialisation including Engineering, pure and applied science health sciences and environmental sciences. The university also has a modern library that is fully computerised.

With these facilities LAUTECH is about the best choice of research-oriented individual and even the industry-focused ones.
